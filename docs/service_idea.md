# AI-based Crypto Investment Alert Service Concept

## Problem
Crypto users receive too much information, but it is difficult to quickly identify which events require attention.

## Service Idea
A user can register watchlist assets and receive risk alerts when abnormal market conditions occur.

## Example Signals
- Price drop
- Volume spike
- Volatility expansion
- Major on-chain movement
- Event-driven market reaction

## User Value
- Faster market awareness
- Better risk recognition
- Personalized watchlist monitoring
- AI-generated event summaries

## Product KPIs
- Watchlist registration rate
- Alert click-through rate
- Return rate within 24 hours after alert
- AI briefing view rate
- Risk checklist usage rate
